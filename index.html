Anime

Anime usually comes in three formats:
TV series: Each episode is about 24 minutes long, there are 12-13 episodes per quarter, and each series must span an integer number of quarters. Anything longer than 2 quarters is considered "long running" or "mainstream crap".
Especially many TV series have OVAs that consist mainly of fanservice, and all hentai are released as OVAs. OVAs are usually 1-6 episodes long, with each episode being about 30 minutes long.
Movies 1-2 hours long, like a live action movie.
Compared to TV, anime movies usually look prettier and are more action-packed.
